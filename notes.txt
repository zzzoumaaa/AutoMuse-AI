App Name:
AutoMuse AI

Problem:
Automotive and transport design students often experience creative blocks and struggle to develop complete vehicle concepts during the early stages of the design process.

Target Users:
- Automotive Design Students
- Transport Design Students
- Concept Artists
- Car Enthusiasts

Features:
- AI Vehicle Concept Generator
- Vehicle Name Generator
- Color Palette Suggestions
- Automotive Design Assistant
- Aerodynamics Suggestions
- Vehicle Structure Recommendations
- Design Language Analysis
- Sketch Feedback/Checker
- Interior Design Suggestions
- Exterior Styling Suggestions
- Future Mobility Ideas
- Materials Recommendations
- Mood Board Inspiration
- Vehicle Segment Suggestions (SUV, Sedan, Hypercar, etc.)
- Sustainability Recommendations
- Design Description Generator

Possible AI Inputs:
- Vehicle Type
- Year
- Style (Cyberpunk, Luxury, Retro, Futuristic)
- Brand Inspiration
- Target Audience
- Country/Market

Example Output:
"The Aether GT is a 2075 electric hypercar featuring active aerodynamic panels, a carbon-fiber monocoque structure, adaptive LED lighting, and a minimalist cockpit inspired by aerospace design."

Tools:
- GitHub
- GitHub Desktop
- Vercel
- AI App Builder
- OpenAI API

- Upload sketches for AI feedback.

- Generate AI mood boards.
