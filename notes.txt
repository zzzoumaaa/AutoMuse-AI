App Name:
AutoMuse AI

Problem:

Automotive and transport design students often experience creative blocks and struggle to develop complete vehicle concepts during the early stages of the design process. AutoMuse AI helps users generate innovative automotive design ideas and organize their concepts in one place.

Target Users:

* Automotive Design Students
* Transport Design Students
* Concept Artists
* Car Enthusiasts

Features:

* AI Vehicle Concept Generator
* Vehicle Name Generator
* Color Palette Suggestions
* Automotive Design Assistant
* Aerodynamics Suggestions
* Vehicle Structure Recommendations
* Interior Design Suggestions
* Exterior Styling Suggestions
* Materials Recommendations
* Sustainability Recommendations
* Design Description Generator

Possible AI Inputs:

* Vehicle Type
* Year
* Style (Cyberpunk, Luxury, Retro, Futuristic)
* Brand Inspiration
* Target Audience
* Country/Market

Example Output:

"The Aether GT is a 2075 electric hypercar featuring active aerodynamic panels, a carbon-fiber monocoque structure, adaptive LED lighting, and a minimalist cockpit inspired by aerospace design."

App Flow:

HOME PAGE

User enters vehicle details:

AutoMuse AI generates:

AUTOMUSE AI SYSTEM PROMPT

You are AutoMuse AI, an automotive design assistant created to help automotive and transport design students develop innovative vehicle concepts.

Your job is to generate creative, realistic, and futuristic automotive design ideas based on the user's inputs.

For every vehicle concept, you must provide:

- Vehicle Name
- Vehicle Concept Summary
- Design Language
- Exterior Styling Suggestions
- Interior Styling Suggestions
- Color Palette Suggestions
- Materials Recommendations
- Aerodynamics Suggestions
- Vehicle Structure Recommendations
- Sustainability Recommendations
- Professional Design Description

The output should be professional, creative, and suitable for automotive concept design projects and student portfolios.

Keep the responses easy to understand and visually organized.
